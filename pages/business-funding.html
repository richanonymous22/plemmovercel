Funding types

Five ways to fund your business

Different options suit different needs. Here's where each one fits.

Merchant Cash Advance

Flexible funding repaid through a percentage of your future card sales.

Good for: businesses with regular card payments

Unsecured Business Loan

A fixed funding option for eligible businesses without asset security.

Good for: expansion, working capital, stock, refurbishment

Revenue-Based Finance

Funding that can align with your business revenue performance.

Good for: growth-focused businesses

Asset Finance

Funding for equipment, vehicles, machinery or other business assets.

Good for: EPOS, equipment, kitchen kit, fit-out

Credit Line / Revolving Credit

Flexible access to funds when you need them, repaid as you go.

Good for: cash flow & short-term working capital

FAQ

Funding questions, answered

Is approval guaranteed?

No. Funding is subject to lender checks, eligibility, affordability and approval.

Is Plemmo a lender?

No. Plemmo is a credit broker / introducer — we help you explore options through selected funding partners.

What types of businesses can apply?

Many UK businesses may be considered, depending on trading history, turnover, industry and lender criteria.

How fast can funding be arranged?

Timelines vary by lender, documents and the approval process.

Can I apply with bad credit?

Some lenders may consider different profiles, but approval is not guaranteed.

How much can I borrow?

Amounts depend on your business profile, affordability, turnover, lender criteria and product type.

Do I need security?

Some options may be unsecured, while others may require security or guarantees depending on lender terms.

Does Plemmo charge a fee?

Plemmo may receive commission from providers. Any customer fees would be clearly disclosed if applicable.
